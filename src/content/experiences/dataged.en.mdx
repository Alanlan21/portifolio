## Context

Dataged specializes in **ECM** (Enterprise Content Management) and **GED** (Electronic Document Management) solutions for the public sector, including clients like the OAB (Brazilian Bar Association) with critical documentation processes.

I joined as an intern in my first semester of college, working in small agile teams with significant technical autonomy from early on.

## Responsibilities

### Legacy System Maintenance

The company's main system was a Java/Spring application with years of production history running for real clients:

- Investigation and fixing of critical production bugs
- Root cause analysis and direct technical support
- Targeted refactoring to improve maintainability

### New Feature Development

Beyond maintenance, I participated in the full cycle of several new modules:

- Requirements gathering with the product team
- Modeling and development through delivery
- Integration with APIs from public sector systems

### Team Context

- Small agile teams with high collaboration
- Version control with Bitbucket
- Project and feature mapping and documentation
